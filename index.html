Technology Policy Lab

Our Mission

The University of Virginia Technology Policy Lab is dedicated to bridging the gap between technological innovation and thoughtful policy-making. We bring together researchers, policymakers, and industry leaders to address the most pressing challenges at the intersection of technology and society.

Research Areas

Artificial Intelligence & Ethics

Exploring the ethical implications of AI systems and developing frameworks for responsible AI deployment in public and private sectors.

Data Privacy & Security

Investigating privacy-preserving technologies and policy approaches to protect individual rights in the digital age.

Digital Infrastructure

Examining broadband access, digital equity, and the policy frameworks needed to ensure inclusive technological development.

Platform Governance

Analyzing the role of digital platforms in society and developing policy recommendations for content moderation and competition.
